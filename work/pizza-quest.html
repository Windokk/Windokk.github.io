My first game ever : Pizza Quest

Full "Game"
Project Overview
In 2015, my father introduced me to Scratch. I used it to create short videos initially. After about a year of experimenting, I started working on my very first project, Pizza Quest. It's a simple game where you play as a soldier trying to cook a pizza. You go through different levels using portals to collect pizza ingredients. Along the way, you encounter healthy legumes that you can defeat with your rifle. Though the game was straightforward, it was a great learning experience for me in creating a project from start to finish. After finishing this game, I switched from Scratch to making short films and videos in my free time.
