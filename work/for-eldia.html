Project Overview
"Attack on Titan" (AoT) is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Hajime Isayama. It is set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story revolves around the adventures of Eren Yeager and his friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the mysteries surrounding their existence. In 2020, during the quarantine, I started watching the animated serie of Aot. After binge-watching it, I really wanted to reproduce the iconic three-dimensional grappling system of this universe. Therefore, I started making a prototype of a grappling system in UE4. It was a big challenge for me because it was the first months of my utilization of this engine. I then contacted a friend of mine who made a 3D model of my favorite character (Armin Arlert) and the prototype was ready !
"Attack on Titan : For Eldia" - Windokk (2020)
